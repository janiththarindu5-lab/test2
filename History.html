Our History

 

Welcome to the story of Seetha Hotel — a journey built on passion, dedication, and over 25 years of experience in hospitality.

Founded with a vision to create a warm and welcoming place for travelers, Seetha Hotel began as a humble establishment focused on comfort, cleanliness, and genuine service. Over the years, we have grown and evolved, continuously improving our facilities and services while staying true to our core values.

What started as a small hotel has now become a trusted destination for guests seeking quality stays and personalized care. Our 25-year journey has been shaped by the relationships we’ve built with our guests, many of whom return time and again, making Seetha Hotel their home away from home.

Throughout the years, we have embraced change and modernization, upgrading our amenities to meet the needs of today’s travelers while preserving the warmth and tradition that define us.
